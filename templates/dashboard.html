Bank

Your main link to the lodge bank account.

  • Import CSV exports from online banking when you’re ready to bring new transactions in.
  • Review the statement list, categories, and running balance so the books stay aligned with the bank.
  • Use allocations and virtual accounts to match lodge practice — the Statement page pulls from what you record here.

Cash

Meeting nights and cash in hand.

  • Record cash taken in and paid out around meetings (subs, dining, raffles, and the rest).
  • When cash is banked, tie it through to the bank side so nothing sits unexplained between cash and the account.
  • Keep each meeting’s story clear — it makes the year-end pack and handover much easier.

For members, subs, and dining in detail (including who is behind), use Members. For a full pack for brethren, see Auditors and Statement.
